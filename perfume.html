unfortunately sephora is not a great place for high quality fragrances.

i think a lot of tgirls/cd girls make the mistake that florals are inherently more femme because of floral imagery, but this isn’t always the case. lots of scents will be perceived as feminine.

the fragrance i recommend the most for tgirls who are new to perfume is (in movie perfume) by (designer). it’s got a (notes notes notes notes notes etc). i think it's particularly good for tgirls because it’s not super offensive and quite wearable for beginners

but if you’re committed to a light floral i’d suggest experimenting at bath and body works, then finding what they’re duping and order samples. make sure to try before you buy as splurging on something with a girly bottle is not nearly as rewarding if you don't like the scent.
also some girls need to internalize that we all have different chemistry and not every scent will work on all skin. fragrence is not one size fits all.
